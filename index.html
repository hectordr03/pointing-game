The Pointing Game

OK, here are the rules.

  • You get a chance to point up, down, left or right
  • At the same time the CPU will turn it's head either up, down, left or right
  • If you point in the same direction that the CPU turns it's head, then you get a point!
  • Ohterwise you lose your turn and the CPU gets a shot at you.
  • First player to score 3 points wins!

Press "Start" to play the Pointing game.
